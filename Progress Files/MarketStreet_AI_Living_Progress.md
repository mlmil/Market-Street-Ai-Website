## Current Project Frame

MarketStreet AI is being developed as a local AI hub for the Market Street / East Ventura industrial corridor.

The public-facing concept is a useful local resource:

- Business directory.
- AI readiness scorecard.
- Industry playbooks.
- Corridor intelligence.
- Local case studies.
- Practical AI education.

Spark AI sits behind the hub as the builder/operator and eventual implementation partner.

The goal is not a hard sales campaign yet. The current phase is **seed planting**: build trust, surface pain points, create useful assets, and find where Spark AI can help local businesses before the Olivas Park Drive Extension changes the corridor narrative in 2027.

## Key Strategic Thesis

The Olivas Park Drive Extension is a timing event, not a guaranteed demand boom.

Research findings:

- The project is the **Olivas Park Drive Extension and Levee Project**, not "Levis."
- The project is a **$51M** City of Ventura infrastructure project.
- It includes roughly **4,500 feet** of roadway/utility extension and **5,400 feet** of levee/floodwall.
- Road opening is expected in **early 2027**; full completion is expected in **summer 2027**.
- The 139-acre Olivas Park Specific Plan area creates a future development question.
- Market Street itself is not directly in the construction zone, but it shares the industrial/service geography and may benefit from improved routing, access narrative, and long-term district perception.
- The road removes friction; it does not automatically create demand.
- Ventura County's business climate is mixed: slow growth, housing pressure, constrained industrial land, and small-bay/service-industrial opportunity.

Spark AI's useful angle:

> AI can help local businesses do more with the same crew before the corridor gets more competitive.
